About

Hi there, I'm Dominick Tamras, an upcoming Computer Programmer, Game Designer and Graphic Artist. I've had a background in computer programming since highschool. Currently I'm pursuing a Interactive Entertainment and Game Design degree at the University of Southern California. Learning skills in game development and working in game engines, such as the Unity engine. While I attend school I continue to make projects or attend game jams, improving both my coding and design skills.

On the flip side of things, I've also taken up Graphic Design, creating different projects across both Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop. Some artwork was created as a paid item and others simply personal to improve my skillset.

I've found great passion in both art and programming, so naturally I find myself leaning to contiue a career in game development. Working with a team and coming together to make a project, is exactly the kind of excitement I find in this industry. I'm always learning the newest technologies when I can, so I could be able to design and develop interactive experiences that can be enjoyed globally by a wide range of audiences.

I'm always on the lookout to join a game company, or help potential clients with interesting projects. Be it coding, Game Development or Graphic Design, if you have interest in my skillset, send me an email!
