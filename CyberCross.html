Cyber/Cross

Cyber/Cross is a card-based, fighting game set in the retro futuristic city of Spectrum. Players control Avatars equipped with a unique deck of Skill Cards that allows them to perform powerful moves and abilities during combat. Fights take place in 3-lane stages where players can dodge between lanes to avoid projectiles and obstacles or maneuver through the lanes to get a jump on their opponents. Players fight in 1v1 tournaments between each other to see who will become the next Cyber/Cross world champion.

There are a total of 3 different characters, each a certain archetype relate to fighting games: All-Rounder (Samurai) Grappler/Brawler (Officer) and MixUp/Zoner (Anarchist).Two levels also were created, featuring enviromental hazards that players have to avoid while fighting. Neo Tokyo features cars that occupy 2 lanes and stuns the players if they are hit, knocking them back in the middle lane and The Office Tower has electric floors that spawn in random lanes.

I was the Lead Designer, Level Designer, Shader Artist and Trailer Editor. This game was made as a digital prototype for a class, and was created in the Unity Engine 2019.
